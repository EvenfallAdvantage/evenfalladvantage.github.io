1. Introduction

Welcome to Evenfall Advantage LLC ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our event security guard training platform and services.

By accessing or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account on our platform
  • Enroll in training courses or certification programs
  • Contact us for support or inquiries
  • Subscribe to our newsletters or communications
  • Participate in surveys or evaluations

This information may include:

  • Full name and contact information (email address, phone number, mailing address)
  • Date of birth and government-issued identification numbers (as required for certification)
  • Educational background and work experience
  • Training progress, assessment scores, and certification records
  • Payment and billing information
  • Emergency contact information
  • State-specific licensing or registration information

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our platform, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages viewed, time spent on pages, navigation patterns)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Login timestamps and session information

2.3 Training and Assessment Data

We collect and maintain records of:

  • Course completion status and progress
  • Assessment and examination results
  • Certificate issuance and expiration dates
  • Attendance records for scheduled classes
  • Interactive scenario responses and performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our training platform and educational services
  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account, authenticate your identity, and provide customer support
  • Certification: To issue, track, and verify security guard certifications and credentials
  • Communication: To send you course updates, notifications, certificates, and administrative messages
  • Compliance: To comply with legal requirements, including state-specific security guard training mandates
  • Quality Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, enhance course content, and improve user experience
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or security threats
  • Marketing: To send promotional materials, newsletters, and updates about new courses (with your consent)

4.1 We May Share Your Information With:

  • Regulatory Authorities: State agencies, licensing boards, and law enforcement as required by law or for certification verification
  • Employers and Hiring Agencies: With your consent, we may verify your training completion and certification status to prospective or current employers
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in operating our platform, processing payments, or delivering services
  • Educational Partners: Accredited institutions or certification bodies involved in course delivery or credential issuance
  • Legal Obligations: When required to comply with legal processes, court orders, or governmental requests

4.2 We Do Not:

  • Sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes
  • Share your information with unaffiliated third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security practices include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Restricted access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure data backup and disaster recovery procedures

Important: While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you promptly of any data breach affecting your information as required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Specifically:

  • Training Records: Maintained for at least 7 years as required by industry regulations and certification standards
  • Certification Records: Retained indefinitely for verification and credential validation purposes
  • Account Information: Retained for the duration of your account activity, plus applicable retention periods
  • Payment Information: Retained as required for tax, accounting, and legal compliance

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Portability: Request a copy of your information in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 11. We will respond to your request within 30 days.
