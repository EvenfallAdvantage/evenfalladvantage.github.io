# Code of Conduct

**Evenfall Advantage LLC**
**Effective Date:** April 6, 2026

---

## 1. Purpose

This Code of Conduct establishes the ethical standards and professional behavior expected of all Evenfall Advantage LLC personnel. As a security services company, we are held to the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and accountability.

## 2. Core Values

- **Integrity** — We are honest, transparent, and ethical in all dealings
- **Accountability** — We take responsibility for our actions and their consequences
- **Professionalism** — We maintain the highest standards of conduct and competence
- **Confidentiality** — We protect the privacy and security of all information entrusted to us
- **Respect** — We treat all individuals with dignity, fairness, and respect

## 3. Professional Standards

### 3.1 Client Relations
- Act in the best interest of clients at all times
- Maintain strict confidentiality of client information
- Deliver services as promised and communicate proactively about any issues
- Never misrepresent capabilities, qualifications, or certifications

### 3.2 Data and Privacy
- Handle personal data with care and in accordance with privacy policies
- Access only the data necessary for your role
- Report any suspected data breach or privacy violation immediately
- Never use customer data for personal purposes

### 3.3 Workplace
- Maintain a professional, respectful, and inclusive environment
- Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or retaliation
- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to management
- Comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and licensing requirements

### 3.4 Technology
- Follow the Acceptable Use Policy for all company systems
- Protect intellectual property and proprietary information
- Report security vulnerabilities through proper channels
- Do not circumvent security controls or access unauthorized systems

## 4. Reporting

Report violations of this Code of Conduct to:
- Direct supervisor or manager
- CTO (James Ferguson) — contact@evenfalladvantage.com
- Anonymous reporting (when available)

Reports will be investigated promptly and confidentially. Retaliation against good-faith reporters is strictly prohibited.

## 5. Acknowledgment

All personnel are required to read, understand, and acknowledge this Code of Conduct upon onboarding and annually thereafter.
