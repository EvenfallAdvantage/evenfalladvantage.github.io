* ## After initial call

* ## Before deep work begins 

## **What it does:**

* ## Aligns expectations 

* ## Shows professionalism 

* ## Establishes control early 

* ## Prevents confusion later

# **2️⃣ MISSION**

One clean sentence:

“Evenfall Advantage will \[what you are doing\] for \[client\] at \[location\] on \[date\] in order to \[purpose\].”

---

### **Example:**

“Evenfall Advantage will conduct a security assessment and develop an operational framework for \[Client\] at \[Venue\] to ensure safe, controlled event operations.”

---
