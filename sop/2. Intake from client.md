When you say “Receive the Mission” (TLP Step 1), in a consulting context, that mission is pulled from the contract / agreement \+ initial conversations.

Your goal is to extract just enough to issue a WARNO and start planning. Not everything.

**\!\!First impression as a system-builder\!\!**

You are building:

WHO — WHAT — WHERE — WHEN — WHY

### **Pull from contract:**

* Client name

* Type of service requested

* Location(s)

* Dates / duration

* Purpose of operation

---

### **Output Example:**

“Evenfall Advantage conducts a security assessment and operational planning support for \[Client\] at \[Location\] from \[Dates\] to ensure safe and controlled event operations.”

* ☐ Who is the client?

* ☐ What are we being asked to do?

* ☐ Where is it happening?

* ☐ When does it occur?

* ☐ Why does the client want this?

* ☐ What is in scope?

* ☐ What is out of scope?

* ☐ What constraints exist?

* ☐ What resources already exist?

* ☐ Who is in charge?

* ☐ What does success look like?
