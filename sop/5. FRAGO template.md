# **HOW TO USE THIS (CRITICAL)**

## **When to issue a FRAGO:**

* Something changes mid-operation

* Risk increases

* Staffing shifts

* Incident occurs

* Plan no longer fits reality

---

## **How to deliver:**

* Verbally first (if urgent)

* Follow with written FRAGO

* Keep it SHORT

---

# **⚠️ RULES FOR FRAGO**

✔ Only include what changed

✔ Be direct

✔ No long explanations

✔ Focus on action
